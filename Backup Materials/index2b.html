Last Opinions:
  • Chicote Club, Madrid, Spain

    Sometimes, we underestimate day-to-day utensils when it comes to their ability to perform well at solving tasks they weren't designed for. I say this because I almost felt like using a spoon to cut through my steak, because the knives were just as useless. The steak was good though...

  • Delirium Café, Brussels, Belgium

    I'm going to be completely honest with you. I had heard about the place and I was pretty stocked to visit it with some friends. They ordered the beers they wanted me to try, and somehow, I couldn't notice how heavily graded they were... I can't remember anything else from that night. 10/10.

  • Bar Paco, Murcia, Spain

    Paco, the owner of the bar, was quite an element... His humorous comments made me feel just like if I were at home on Christmas Eve, surrounded by phony relatives who I barely know. The place wasn't very clean either. Don't go there expecting good food.
